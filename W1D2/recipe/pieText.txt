Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

lemon meringue pie

One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

    1 cup white sugar
    2 tbsp all-purpose flour
    3 tbsp cornstarch
    1/4 tsp salt
    1 1/2 cups water
    2 lemons, juiced and zested
    2 tbsp butter
    4 egg yolks, beaten
    1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
    4 egg whites
    6 tbsp white sugar
